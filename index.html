Our history

Founded as a student-led initiative at Eduvos Mbombela Campus in early 2025, BitLabs was created to bridge the gap between theoretical IT education and practical, real-world problem solving.

What started as a small group of passionate developers has grown strategically into an organised tech lab with four Special Interest Groups, delivering impactful solutions for both academic and industry challenges.

Today, BitLabs is recognised for its commitment to enduring worth — combining technical excellence, ethical research, and personalised mentorship to shape future IT leaders.

About us

BitLaps is more than a club — it's an innovation lab. We believe in learning by doing. Our members excel academically and collaborate on projects that solve actual problems, from AI summarizers to data dashboards.

We focus on four core pillars: Leadership, Innovation & Application Development, Research & Ethics, and Learner Induction. Each SIG operates with corporate-grade standards, ensuring members graduate with portfolios that reflect real engineering capability.
